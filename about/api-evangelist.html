API Evangelist is the working toolbox of Kin Lane, designed to explore and make sense of the technology, business, policies, and people involved in creating and consuming HTTP APIs. The site serves as an evolving repository of information that covers nearly every aspect of API operations. The goal of the site is to help enterprises, startups, analysts, consultants, and other stakeholders assess, manage, and govern the increasingly expansive API landscape that powers various business sectors.

This stie is built on 15 years of experience working with APIs, compiles insights, artifacts, and videos to document and analyze the intersection of technology, business, policies, and people shaping the API ecosystem. API Evangelist works to provide practical, hands-on knowledge that can be applied directly to operations.
